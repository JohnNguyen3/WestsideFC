John W’s 2 Goals Propels #2 Westside FC Over Rivals #6 Small Goals Big Hearts In Rout to Second SGS D3 Finals Appearance

#2 Westside FC, continues their dominant run in the playoffs with a commanding 3-0 victory over #6 Small Goals Big Hearts, securing their second SGS Division 3 Finals appearance.

Led by the red-hot form of John W, who now boasts a record-breaking six goals and one assist in the playoffs, #2 Westside FC extended their clean sheet streak to four games and their overall win streak to five. Their MVP goalkeeper, Kaz, once again demonstrated why #2 Westside FC has the top-rated defense in the league, completely stifling #6 Small Goals Big Hearts’ offense.

Playing without their star player Joe, #6 Small Goals Big Hearts struggled to mount any serious attacking threats, leaving a noticeable void in their game plan.

John W wasted no time asserting his influence, netting the first goal of the match in the 3rd minute after stealing the ball from a defender and slotting it home to give #2 Westside FC an early 1-0 lead.

In the 6th minute, a quick out-of-bounds play from Travie down the right sideline led to a spectacular backheel pass from Niko to John W, who calmly finished the play to double #2 Westside FC’s lead to 2-0.

#2 Westside FC would continue to put the game out of reach with an 8th-minute goal from Baki, assisted by John W, to make it 3-0. From that point, #6 Small Goals Big Hearts couldn’t recover, as they struggled to create scoring opportunities against a suffocating #2 Westside FC defense.

Kaz and the #2 Westside backline held firm throughout, ensuring a shutout and securing the team’s sixth consecutive victory over #6 Small Goals Big Hearts, bringing their all-time record to 6-0-3.

What’s Next: The Final Test

#2 Westside FC will face off against #1 Redmonk United in the Small Goal Soccer Division 3 Championship Match on September 18 at Hartman Field. Kickoff is scheduled for 9:25 PM Pacific Time.

Both teams are bringing the league’s top defenses into the title match, with #2 Westside FC boasting the #1 defense and #3 offense, while #1 Redmonk United counters with the #2 defense and the league’s top-ranked offense.

The two teams met earlier in the season, with #2 Westside FC narrowly edging out #1 Redmonk United in a thrilling 2-1 victory, thanks to a last-second goal from Marcos. Since then, #1 Redmonk United has gone undefeated, making them a formidable opponent in what promises to be a highly competitive and exciting championship match.

#2 Westside FC enters with a 2-1-0 all-time record against #1 Redmonk United, but with everything on the line, they will need to be at their best to capture the title.
