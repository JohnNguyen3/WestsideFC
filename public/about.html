Welcome to the Westside FC Team Page!

We are a passionate and dedicated soccer team committed to excellence on and off the field. Our team consists of skilled players based in the greater Seattle-area who share a love for the beautiful game.

Our Mission

Our mission is to cultivate a culture of excellence and development within the realm of soccer, aspiring to set the gold standard for the sport worldwide. At the heart of our endeavor lies a profound commitment to nurturing and honing the talents of both our players and those in the wider soccer community. We aim not only to achieve success on the field but also to inspire and empower children everywhere to pursue their passion for the beautiful game. Through dedication, innovation, and a relentless pursuit of improvement, we strive to leave an indelible mark on the soccer landscape, serving as a beacon of inspiration for generations to come.
