Westside FC Match Report 5/16: Jose's Pair of Goals Sparks Westside FC's 5 Goal Rally From Down 0-2 to Defeat Korean Barbieque

Westside FC continued their impressive form with a remarkable comeback victory against Korean Barbieque, extending their unbeaten streak to six matches and win streak to five. The return of Thong, Manuel, Marcos, John N, and Travie proved pivotal in bolstering Westside FC's long-term fitness and depth for the match.

The game started in favor of Korean Barbieque, capitalizing on a defensive lapse from Westside FC in the 4th minute to take a 1-0 lead. Just three minutes later, they doubled their lead with a swift counterattack, exploiting a one-on-one opportunity with Travie in goal.

However, Korean Barbieque’s 10th ranked defense’s inability to defend reared its ugly head and Westside FC would exploit it.

Jose would sub on right after Korean Barbieque’s 2nd goal; his impactful presence was felt immediately upon entering the pitch. In the 9th minute, his incredible individual effort resulted in a goal from 15 yards out, cutting the deficit in half.

John W joined the scoring frenzy in the 11th minute, converting an opportunistic cross from Marcos to equalize the match. Within six minutes, Westside FC turned the tide, taking the lead in the 13th minute with a goal from Baki off a rebounded shot from Thong.

The score would remain 3-2 with Westside FC taking all the momentum and confidence from Korean Barbieque going into the half.

Korean Barbieque continued to fight as in the 30th minute John N would make a crucial one-on-one sliding save to deny Korean Barbieque an opportunity to level the score.

Jose would then secure his 2nd goal a minute later in the 31st minute, capitalizing on a deflected cross to double Westside FC's lead. Baki then orchestrated a terrific pass to Thong in the 33rd minute, sealing the team's 5-2 victory with a decisive goal.

With this win, Westside FC maintains an unbeaten record against Korean Barbieque in their three matchups.
