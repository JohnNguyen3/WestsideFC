Westside FC Match Report 4/20: #5 Westside FC Survives Against Rivals #8 Small Goals Big Hearts in a High-Scoring Thriller

In the sixth encounter between #5 Westside FC and #8 Small Goals Big Hearts, the teams delivered another closely contested match, adding another chapter to their intense rivalry.

Previously, Westside FC dominated possession and limited Small Goals Big Hearts to a single goal in their first championship appearance. However, this meeting proved to be a high-scoring affair, with both teams combining for seven goals.

The scoring frenzy began in the 8th minute when Baki initiated the attack with a brilliant pass from John W. However, Small Goals Big Hearts quickly responded in the 13th minute, capitalizing on a deflected pass from a Westside FC free kick to level the score.

Niko swiftly reclaimed the lead for #5 Westside FC with a remarkable strike in the 14th minute. By the 16th minute, a misjudged pass by the Small Goals Big Hearts goalkeeper resulted in another goal for Westside FC, extending their lead to 3-1 going into half.

Despite the lead, Peter Favour's shoulder injury forced him out of the game, impacting the team's defense. In the second half, DJ went in goal and played terrifically, but Small Goals Big Hearts capitalized on defensive lapses, with Lefty Morris (Joe) spearheading the attack.

Lefty Morris's relentless efforts paid off, as he scored two goals in the 26th minute and the 32nd minute to level the score at 3-3. However, Niko's crucial goal off a cross from John N in the 37th minute secured the victory for #5 Westside FC.

The remainder of the match saw Westside FC adopt a defensive stance, with DJ making a decisive 11th save on a one-on-one save opportunity in the final moments to preserve the win. With this victory, Westside FC extended their win streak to four and their unbeaten streak to six matches.

Westside FC and Small Goals Big Hearts have now split their six encounters evenly, with Westside FC emerging victorious in their last three meetings.
